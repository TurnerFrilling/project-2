In 2013, Craig Jones and Eric Hyatt took over Angelo’s Pizza on East Sixth Avenue, giving the space and menu an overhaul and renaming it Angelo’s Taverna.

Pizza and pasta stayed true to the original, which was opened by the Laveo family in 1974, but oysters and housemade limoncello were among the additions that attracted a new generation of fans. And now Angelo’s Taverna has a younger sibling that just opened at 6885 South Santa Fe Drive in Littleton — and it includes the attached Carbory Winery; both projects are from Jones and partner/chef Gabriel Aragon.
